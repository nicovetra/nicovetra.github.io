Mountain Clouds

Poem · February 2, 2026
Have you ever been so high, that distant clouds form mountains. The land parcelled and torn into innumerable grids as far as the eyes can see. Up here you can't feel the wind blow just the hum drum of humanities toys shrinking the world. In the distance the mountains below become little specks of dirt and rock where still rivers flow. But the real mountains glisten pink in a soft glow transforming and changing peaks rising and falling I see formations I would never see erupt and explode. And quietly I sit to watch it unfold.
